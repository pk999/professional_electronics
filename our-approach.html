Our Approach

We are the best in market

Every property is unique, so there may be a few extra steps involved in getting things done. We are required to complete the following steps to get you connected. Once we have your order, we can discuss the installation process specific to your property in more detail.

Agree your install plan

When we receive the order from you, we arrange for our professional technician to meet you, make a plan, talk to you about the work requirements and get a mutual agreement on the installation.

The initial meeting can take up to an hour, and the work that needs to be completed will be noted on an agreement form completed by our technician, which you’ll be asked to sign.

This agreement covers all the work that will be done and installing the required types of equipment on the property. Our technician will also discuss the build and connect components with you.

Build your network

Our installation team will come and go according to the plan which we have agreed upon, and just in case if we do need to make any changes to what was agreed, we will make sure to take consign from you before changing anything. You don’t need to be at the site, but it helps if you are contactable by phone.

Connect you

Once we’ve built the network and installed all the required devices, you need to be there so that our technician will walk through the plan and explain how they can be operated before leaving the site.
